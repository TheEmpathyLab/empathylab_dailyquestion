Empathy Lab

Empathy Lab has one goal,
to drastically improve the way we build and share empathy.

To do this, Empathy Lab is on a mission to create an environment where stories can be exchanged, understanding can be gathered, anonymous vitriol is impossible, and feelings can be shared. By having these conversations, we improve our social awareness and empathy for different people.

In an effort to build with end users in mind, we are currently moving into phase two of the lab, Research and Design. Follow our progress on Twitter where we will continue to post questions and share articles about empathy. More great things to come in 2018!
